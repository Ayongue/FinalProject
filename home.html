Iota Xi.

Delta Sigma Phi (ΔΣΦ) is a prominent social fraternity founded on December 10, 1899, at the City College of New York. The fraternity's core values are promoting the ideals of friendship, scholarship, and service to others. With a mission to cultivate men of excellence, Delta Sigma Phi encourages its members to uphold high standards of leadership, integrity, and academic achievement. Over the years, it has grown into a nationwide organization with chapters across universities and colleges in the United States. Known for its commitment to community service and fostering lifelong bonds among its members, Delta Sigma Phi has played a significant role in shaping leaders in various fields, from business to public service. Its distinctive rituals, symbols, and traditions contribute to the sense of brotherhood that defines the fraternity.

Delta Sigma Phi Fraternity

Delta Sigma Phi is a nonprofit organization that creates friendships, builds leaders and expands campus opportunities. By joining Delta Sig, you become part of a group that helps you achieve success through academic, social, civic, and professional development. We work in partnership with more than 100 campuses across the United States, complementing the collegiate experience by facilitating positive relationships with other students, campus professionals and community leaders. We work to provide you with a strong friend group, your first professional network, unique life experiences, and lifelong educational opportunities.



Today’s college experience provides a number of opportunities for connection and growth. You can join student government, intramural sports teams, honor societies, service organizations, or professional clubs. However, fraternities remain the best organizations for meeting students that are involved with all aspects of campus life. Additionally, fraternities are designed with lifelong affiliation in mind. Meaning, the connections you make in college will last long after graduation, and the network you form will continue to expand as you collaborate with students and alumni from other colleges and universities. Simply stated, if you are eager to meet people with a wide variety of interests who are all looking to become the best versions of themselves, then we encourage you to learn more about Delta Sigma Phi Fraternity.

Philanthropy

Delta Sigma Phi has a strong partnership with the American Red Cross, actively supporting its mission through a variety of charitable efforts. One of the primary ways the fraternity contributes is by hosting blood drives on campus, encouraging students and community members to donate blood and make a difference in the lives of those in need. In addition to these blood drives, Delta Sigma Phi raises funds through philanthropy events, donating a portion of the money earned to the American Red Cross to further its life-saving programs and services. This collaboration not only provides crucial support to the organization but also strengthens the fraternity’s commitment to serving others and making a positive impact in the community.



Delta Sigma Phi actively supports the ASPCA (American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals) through various philanthropy events and initiatives. As a chapter, we raise funds during these events, with a portion of the proceeds being donated directly to the ASPCA to support their mission of preventing animal cruelty and providing essential care to animals in need. Additionally, we work to raise awareness about the organization's work by advertising and promoting the ASPCA through social media, flyers, and word-of-mouth, encouraging others to get involved. This partnership allows us to contribute meaningfully to the well-being of animals, while also fostering a sense of community and compassion within our fraternity.
